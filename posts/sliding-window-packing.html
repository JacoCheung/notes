Sliding Window 的 packing 陷阱

LLM Attention 与实现 · Part 2

1. 引言

Sliding Window Attention(SWA)单层只让每个 token 看自己前 $W-1$ 个 token,接口干净:Mistral、Gemma、Phi-3 都靠它把训练序列推到 32K+。但和另一个常见做法 sequence packing(多条短样本拼成一条长序列以省 padding)组合后,会出现一个不易察觉的等价性陷阱:

只靠 SWA "自然分隔"不同样本不安全。 多层 attention 的实际感受野会越过窗口,把别的样本信息混入当前样本。数学上不再等价于独立训练。

下面用 6 token、2 层、$W=3$ 的最小例子拆开这件事,再给出实际可用的修复:FlashAttention 的 cu_seqlens / document mask。

Sliding Window's Packing Trap

LLM Attention and Implementations · Part 2

1. Intro

Sliding Window Attention (SWA) limits each token to its previous $W-1$ neighbors per layer. The interface is clean, and Mistral, Gemma, and Phi-3 all use it to push training context to 32K+. Combined with the other common practice of sequence packing (concatenating short samples into one long sequence to save padding), it has a subtle equivalence trap:

SWA alone does not "naturally separate" samples. The multi-layer effective receptive field reaches past the window and silently mixes information across samples. Mathematically, the result is no longer equivalent to training each sample independently.

The smallest example that exposes the issue uses 6 tokens, 2 layers, and $W=3$. The fix that holds up in practice is FlashAttention's cu_seqlens / document mask.

3. 两层 SWA:实际感受野扩大

加一层。 act₁ 经同一个 SWA 操作得到 act₂。 对 act₂ 的 d,直接 attend 仍是 act₁ 的 $\{b, c, d\}$,单层窗口看起来依然是 3。 但 act₁ 里的 b 上一层已经 attend 过 act₀ 的 $\{a, b\}$(b 在 act₀ 是位置 2,$[b-2, b] = [a, b]$;a 之前没有 token)。 这条路径让 a 的信息也进入 act₂ 的 d。

结果:act₂ 的 d 实际感受野是 act₀ 的 $\{a, b, c, d\}$,而不是 $\{b, c, d\}$。 单层窗口在多层叠加后被放大。

3. Two-layer SWA: the effective receptive field grows

Add a second layer. act₁ goes through the same SWA op and becomes act₂. For d at act₂, the direct attendees are still $\{b, c, d\}$ in act₁, so the per-layer window still looks like 3. But b at act₁ already attended to $\{a, b\}$ from act₀ on the previous layer (b sits at position 2, $[b-2, b] = [a, b]$, clamped at the start). That path carries a's information into d at act₂.

The result: the effective receptive field of d at act₂ is $\{a, b, c, d\}$ in act₀, not $\{b, c, d\}$. The per-layer window is amplified by stacking.

Signal decays sharply across layers, so the model represents distant tokens poorly in practice. The gradient on cross-sample contamination does not care about representation quality: once the ERF crosses a sample boundary, the gradient path exists.

5. Packed training: where the contamination happens

To avoid wasting padding on short samples, training pipelines often concatenate samples into one long sequence. Suppose two samples ABC and BCD, each computing loss on its last token (a simplified causal LM next-token loss). Concatenate them into ABCBCD (6 tokens, same length as abcdef above) and run with $W=3$ causal SWA and 2 layers. Fig 4 takes the D from the second sample at act₂ as the target:

具体追踪:

  • 位置 4 的 B(sample 2)在 act₁ 是用 act₀ 的 [pos 2, pos 3, pos 4] = $\{B_{s1}, C_{s1}, B_{s2}\}$ 算出来的 —— sample 1 的 B 和 C 已经混进去。
  • 位置 6 的 D 在 act₂ 是用 act₁ 的 [pos 4, pos 5, pos 6] = $\{B_{s1+s2 \, \text{mix}}, C_{s2}, D_{s2}\}$ 算的。
  • 结果:sample 1 的 $\{B, C\}$(图里红色块)的梯度沿这条路径传到 D 的 logit,训练梯度不再等于"BCD 独立训练时的梯度"。 A 在 2 层 + $W=3$ 的设置下恰好落在 D 的 ERF 边界外(ERF = $L(W-1)+1 = 5$,A 距 D 5 个 token);再加一层就会被吸进来。

另一面,模型看到的"局部上下文"是两条样本的 token 混合 —— 注意力分布 / 隐藏状态包含跨样本统计,模型可能学到"sample 2 的 B 之后跟 sample 2 的 C"这种偶然 co-occurrence,污染数据分布。

这条结论无法靠调小 $W$ 绕过。 ERF 公式 $L(W-1)+1$ 对 $L$ 和 $W$ 都单调增,缩小 $W$ 只是让每层步长变小,堆够层之后仍然越过样本边界。 反过来,$W$ 越大、$L$ 越深,污染越严重。

Tracing it step by step:

  • Position 4 (B in sample 2) at act₁ is computed from act₀ positions [2, 3, 4] = $\{B_{s1}, C_{s1}, B_{s2}\}$. Sample 1's B and C are already mixed in.
  • Position 6 (D) at act₂ is computed from act₁ positions [4, 5, 6] = $\{B_{s1+s2 \, \text{mixed}}, C_{s2}, D_{s2}\}$.
  • Result: gradient from sample 1's $\{B, C\}$ (red cells in the figure) flows into D's logit; the training gradient is no longer equal to the gradient BCD would receive on its own. A happens to fall just outside D's ERF in this 2-layer, $W=3$ case (ERF = $L(W-1)+1 = 5$ and A sits 5 tokens from D); one more layer pulls it in.

Separately, the "local context" the model sees becomes a mixture of two samples' tokens. Attention distributions and hidden states pick up cross-sample statistics, and the model can latch onto accidental co-occurrences ("sample 2's B is often followed by sample 2's C") that corrupt the underlying data distribution.

Shrinking $W$ does not bypass the problem. The ERF formula $L(W-1)+1$ is monotonic in both $L$ and $W$; a smaller $W$ only reduces the per-layer step, and enough layers still cross the boundary. Larger $W$ and deeper $L$ make the leakage worse, not the other way around.

6. 正确做法:document mask / cu_seqlens

必须显式告诉 attention "样本边界在哪",让跨边界的 attention 分数变成 $-\infty$。 在 attention 矩阵层面,等价于把矩阵切成块对角(block-diagonal):

      A B C B C D
   A [• . . . . .]    sample 1 内部:三角形
   B [• • . . . .]
   C [• • • . . .]
   B [. . . × . .]    跨样本的 attention = 0
   C [. . . • • .]    sample 2 内部:三角形
   D [. . . • • •]
        ↑
   sample 1 / 2 边界,× 全部 mask 掉

FlashAttention 的 flash_attn_varlen_func 原生支持,用 cu_seqlens(cumulative sequence lengths)告诉 kernel 切在哪:

from flash_attn import flash_attn_varlen_func

# 两条样本:ABC (长度 3) 和 BCD (长度 3),拼成 6-token
cu_seqlens = torch.tensor([0, 3, 6], dtype=torch.int32)

out = flash_attn_varlen_func(
    q, k, v,
    cu_seqlens_q=cu_seqlens,
    cu_seqlens_k=cu_seqlens,
    max_seqlen_q=3, max_seqlen_k=3,
    causal=True,
    window_size=(2, 0),    # 因果 + SWA,W=3 → left=W-1=2
)

FA kernel 内部把每段 K/V 当成独立 attention 计算,块对角之外的 K/V block 连 load 都不做,数学上严格等价于"分别 forward 两条序列",计算量也按各自序列长度算。

Document mask + SWA 是 Mistral / Mixtral 训练栈的标准配方,Megatron-LM 通过 reset_attention_mask=True + reset_position_ids=True 自动构造,HF Transformers 通过 attention_mask + 段 ID 显式传递。

6. The fix: document mask / cu_seqlens

You must explicitly tell attention "where the sample boundaries are" and force cross-boundary attention scores to $-\infty$. At the attention-matrix level this is equivalent to making the matrix block-diagonal:

      A B C B C D
   A [• . . . . .]    inside sample 1: a triangle
   B [• • . . . .]
   C [• • • . . .]
   B [. . . × . .]    cross-sample attention = 0
   C [. . . • • .]    inside sample 2: a triangle
   D [. . . • • •]
        ↑
   sample 1 / 2 boundary, × is masked out

FlashAttention's flash_attn_varlen_func supports this natively via cu_seqlens (cumulative sequence lengths):

from flash_attn import flash_attn_varlen_func

# Two samples: ABC (len 3) and BCD (len 3), packed into a 6-token batch
cu_seqlens = torch.tensor([0, 3, 6], dtype=torch.int32)

out = flash_attn_varlen_func(
    q, k, v,
    cu_seqlens_q=cu_seqlens,
    cu_seqlens_k=cu_seqlens,
    max_seqlen_q=3, max_seqlen_k=3,
    causal=True,
    window_size=(2, 0),    # causal + SWA, W=3 → left=W-1=2
)

The FA kernel treats each K/V segment as an independent attention computation. K/V blocks outside the block-diagonal are not even loaded. The math is strictly equivalent to "forward each sequence separately," and the compute matches per-sequence cost.

Document mask + SWA is the standard recipe in the Mistral / Mixtral training stack. Megatron-LM constructs it automatically via reset_attention_mask=True + reset_position_ids=True. HF Transformers passes it explicitly via the attention_mask with segment IDs.

7. 实操 checklist

配置等价于独立训练?说明
拼接 + 普通因果 mask❌跨样本完全可见(连一层就漏)
拼接 + 因果 + SWA(单层)⚠️短样本"看起来"等价;堆层后失效
拼接 + 因果 + SWA(多层)❌本节例子,ERF ≈ $L(W-1)+1$ 跨边界
拼接 + cu_seqlens(document mask)✅块对角,严格等价
拼接 + cu_seqlens + SWA✅Mistral 训练的标准配方

怎么自查:

  • 训练框架:
    • Megatron-LM:打开 --reset-attention-mask --reset-position-ids;数据 pipeline 用 <eod> 切样本
    • HF Transformers:检查 position_ids 是否在样本边界归零(0,1,2,0,1,2,...),而不是单调递增;attention_mask 是否含段信息
    • NeMo / Megatron-Core:用 packed_seq_params 传 cu_seqlens
    • 直接用 FA:确认调用的是 flash_attn_varlen_func 而不是 flash_attn_func
  • debug 方法:训练 step 中 print 一下 attention mask 的形状 / 非零分布。整张 mask 是单一三角形,基本可以判定 document mask 没生效;正确的块对角应该在样本边界处看到明显的"断"。
  • RoPE 位置编码:每段样本的 position_ids 必须从 0 重启,否则就算 attention 切对了,RoPE 也会让 sample 2 用 "全局位置 3"的 phase rotation,跟独立训练时 sample 2 用"局部位置 0"不等价。
  • loss mask:padding token 和不算 loss 的位置在 loss 上 mask 掉是另一回事,跟 attention mask 互相独立 —— 两者都要正确。

一行总结:packing 训练用 SWA 时,必须同时挂 cu_seqlens 或 segment-aware attention_mask;SWA 本身不切样本。

7. Practical checklist

ConfigurationEquivalent to independent training?Notes
Concat + plain causal mask❌Cross-sample fully visible (leaks even in one layer)
Concat + causal + SWA (1 layer)⚠️Looks equivalent for short samples; fails with depth
Concat + causal + SWA (multi-layer)❌This note's example; ERF ≈ $L(W-1)+1$ crosses boundary
Concat + cu_seqlens (document mask)✅Block-diagonal, strictly equivalent
Concat + cu_seqlens + SWA✅Standard Mistral training recipe

How to self-check:

  • Training framework:
    • Megatron-LM: turn on --reset-attention-mask --reset-position-ids; the data pipeline cuts samples on <eod>
    • HF Transformers: verify position_ids reset to 0 at sample boundaries (0,1,2,0,1,2,...) rather than running monotonically; verify attention_mask carries segment info
    • NeMo / Megatron-Core: pass cu_seqlens via packed_seq_params
    • Calling FA directly: confirm you call flash_attn_varlen_func, not flash_attn_func
  • Debug: print the attention mask's shape and nonzero distribution mid-step. A single triangle is a strong sign the document mask was dropped. A correct block-diagonal mask shows clear "breaks" at sample boundaries.
  • RoPE: per-sample position_ids must restart from 0; otherwise even with attention sliced correctly, RoPE applies sample-2 at "global position 3" rather than "local position 0," and the equivalence breaks at the positional encoding layer.
  • Loss mask: masking padding tokens or non-loss positions out of the loss is a separate concern from the attention mask; both have to be correct independently.

One-line summary: when packing under SWA, cu_seqlens or a segment-aware attention_mask must also be passed. SWA alone does not slice samples.

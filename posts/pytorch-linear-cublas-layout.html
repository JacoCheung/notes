torch linear 和 layout

torch 视角 text cuBLAS 视角 layout reduce axis remark
\(x * w^t\) 前向 \(w^t * x\) tn k (input dim)
\(\mathrm{d}w^t = x^t * \mathrm{d}y\) 反向 \(\mathrm{d}w = x * \mathrm{d}y^t\) nt m (batch) 实际要求的是 \(\mathrm{d}w = \mathrm{d}y^t * x\)
\(\mathrm{d}x = \mathrm{d}y * w^{tt}\) 反向 \(\mathrm{d}x = w * \mathrm{d}y\) nn n (output dim)
💡The torch shape and the cuBLAS shape are transposed. So \(A * B\) in torch becomes \(B * A\) in cuBLAS.
